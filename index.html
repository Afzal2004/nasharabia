Who We Are

Built on Reliability. Trusted Across Saudi Arabia.

Nash Arabia General Contracting Co. is a Saudi-based provider of manpower, equipment and industrial trading services, supporting construction, oil & gas, and infrastructure projects with a single point of accountability from mobilization to closeout.

Our teams are vetted, our fleet is maintained to strict standards, and our supply chain is built to keep your site running without delay — because in this industry, downtime costs more than diligence.

  • Fully licensed & compliant with Saudi labour law
  • Rapid mobilization across Eastern Province & beyond
  • Dedicated account managers for every contract
