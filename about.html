About Us

Our Story

A Reliable Partner for Every Site in the Kingdom

Nash Arabia General Contracting Co. was established to close the gap between site demand and dependable supply. Based in Al‑Jubail, we work alongside contractors, developers and industrial operators to provide the manpower, equipment and materials that keep projects on schedule.

Our approach is straightforward: understand the site requirement, mobilize quickly, and hold ourselves to the same safety and quality standard we would expect on our own projects. That discipline is why clients return to us project after project.
