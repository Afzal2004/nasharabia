Our Story

A Saudi Company, Built for Heavy Industry

Nash Arabia General Contracting Co. was founded to answer a simple need in the Saudi market: a single, accountable partner for manpower, equipment and industrial trading — one that could mobilise quickly and deliver reliably.

Since then we have grown into a multi-disciplinary contractor supporting construction, oil & gas and infrastructure clients across the Kingdom, with a head office in Riyadh and mobilisation teams that reach every major project region.

Every engagement is guided by the same principles: safety without compromise, quality that meets international benchmarks, and a workforce treated as our greatest asset.
